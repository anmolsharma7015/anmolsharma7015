## Social Media DO's

* DO: Keep your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles professional — recruiters actively search these platforms.
* DO: Think before you post. Ask yourself: would I be comfortable if my professor or future employer saw this?
* DO: Use privacy settings on personal platforms (Instagram, Snapchat) to control who sees your content.
* DO: Credit others when you share their work, ideas, or images online.
* DO: Report cyberbullying or harassment using the platform's built-in reporting tools immediately.

## Social Media DONT's

* DON'T: Post complaints about college, faculty, or classmates publicly — this can have serious academic consequences.
* DON'T: Share your college ID, registration number, or personal address on any public platform.
* DON'T: Accept connection requests from people you do not know personally, especially on professional platforms.
* DON'T: Spread unverified news or forward messages without checking the source from a credible outlet.
* DON'T: Use social media to settle personal disputes — always resolve conflicts privately and respectfully.
